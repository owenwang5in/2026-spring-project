Learning to do reasearch──꒰✉️꒱ ❞ ‧₊˚

This semester, our research topic was why dog adoption rates are low. First, we used tools such as Fishbone Analysis and the 5 Whys Analysis to identify possible causes and develop our survey questions. Next, we selected suitable locations and conducted surveys by interviewing members of the public. After collecting the data, we organized and analyzed it in Statistics class. We created charts to present the results and explained what we believed the data showed. Through this research project, I improved my data organization and analysis skills, and I learned how to collect and interpret information more effectively.
