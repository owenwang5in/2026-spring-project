Dog story

In a hidden magic city where witches and humans live together, peace is getting less and less, humans are hunting the witches. In this world lives Will, a magical dog with black fur and a purple smoky aura around him. He is brave, calm, and stable. But often hides when danger appears because he fears others see him as cursed. As humans enter the magical city, they start targeting anything connected to magic, so Will is mistaken for a witch because of his purple aura. With more hunters closing in on him, Will was forced to search for ways to change his fate. As danger grows, Will slowly changes. At first he’s afraid to let others see his purple aura, even start doubting himself that he’s cursed. So he tried to hide behind anything. But over time, he realises running is not enough, and he starts doing what he can to prove he is not harmful and to help others instead of only protecting himself. He grows from a scared, hiding dog into someone that can even protect others. But then, the problem gets worse as more humans enter the city and hunt the witches. Will can’t hide himself anymore. Eventually, a mysterious stranger takes him to a shop that can change his aura color to help him escape danger. The attack just starts, people hunting every witch they can find, and the situation is getting serious. Unfortunately, the mysterious shop that changed Will’s aura is destroyed, and his true purple smoky glow appears again. As fire spreads and buildings collapse, Will stops running away for the first time. Even though he is scared, he uses his magic to protect both witches and humans, guiding lost children through smoke and saving people from falling buildings. A child he rescues tells the crowd the truth about him, and the humans slowly realize they have been wrong to hunt him. After the attack, the magical city is no longer safe. The witches and humans are forced to leave to find a new home, but Will becomes separated from them during the escape. He’s now alone. He has to stay himself through the rainy streets of the human city. Even though people still stare at his strange purple aura, Will no longer hides it. The human city was cold, crowded, and unfamiliar to Will. With no one left beside him, he wandered the streets alone until exhaustion forced him to rest in an abandoned alley. As rain poured down, purple smoke slowly rose around his body. A nearby shelter worker noticed the strange glow and carefully followed it through the darkness. There, hidden in the corner, they discovered Will trembling in his sleep. The workers see Will’s kindness in his eyes, so chose to save him and brought him to the shelter.
