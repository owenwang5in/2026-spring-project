What is good?

For what is good, the main point is to think about life choices. I think we all should think about the decisions we make in our life. Most people make different daily habits and social interactions every day without thinking too much about them. The reason behind it resides that they believe their alternatives are fine or maybe they just want to flee from the sad past. However, if people truly want a better life, I recommend thinking about actions and learning from past mistakes. I’m sure that you don’t want to keep messing up with something like before you always did, how about staying calm and thinking for a sec? Thinking about your life's choices can let you improve your discernment in the future, because after you think about your wrong options in the past, you will know what you or others did wrong, then you will get a chance to evolve yourself. If you think about your life’s choices, you won’t be acting with intentionality. Regretting a lot will make you keep getting sad. If you keep getting sad, you will develop depression. In the future you will regret more and start circling with this. But still, remember to think about your life at the right time, like when break time. Think is for helping people have time to relax and think clearly about their assortments, not for not paying attention in class. Last, to make a clear comparison between the people who are lazy about their life and people that are well on thinking about their life, let’s all try to reflect on our life choices and make better decisions. To put it simply, paying attention and thinking about life choices is good.
