About our research

Study Design
To study design, at first we watched a movie called Twelve Nights. After that we went to the shelter and got information about our survey from professional people. Then we start finding our research topic by making a fish bone note, create 5 whys for our notes and find action points (advertisement of the shelter is not effective) for the ideas. The last part of survey design is to come up with research questions. We did online research first and brainstormed the Q, after Q came out we put them together and discussed them with the instructor. When the questions were mostly finished, we went to revise with instructor advice.

Participants
For the participants, we interviewed 79 people. There are 15 people from Daan, 10 students and 5 teachers. 50 people is the minimum.

Materials/Tools
The tools and materials that we used included a question list, camera, stationery, poster, and costume.

Procedure
Our procedure was to first form the groups, then assign jobs such as speaker, recorder or helper. After everything's ready, we went to the place where the survey concluded. When we finished the survey concluding we conduct the survey by introducing ourselves and project, then collect data with face to face means last ask age, dont ask age before any question

Special Considerations
When we are giving the survey, we need to speak loudly, find a good place and dont ask more than one person at the same time. Don’t switch jobs and change the introduction, we need unity. Be respectful, be like a normal person.

Data Analysis
When we finished the survey, we started analyzing the data. First we processed the data (Steps that organize the data so it is easy to read and see patterns) by compiling the answers, sorting the data and visualizing the data. Then we started analyzing the data (Explanation of important things we see when we look at the data) by describing the data because the results are region specific, at last we conclude the data.
