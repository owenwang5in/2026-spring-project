What is good

What is Good? What is good? I think good is being kind and not hurting people. A good person helps others and make people feel safe. One example is not bully. Bully is bad, so not bullying is good. First, bully makes people sad and scared. When people get bullied, they feel very unhappy. They will feel scared every day. Some people think bully is funny, but it is not funny about the person who gets hurt. A good person should not make others feel sad or scared. Second, bullying makes people have no friends. If a person bullies others, no one wants to play with him or her. Other people may also say, “Don't be friends with that person.” Then the bad bully will have no friends. Good people should be kind, so they can have good relationships with others. Third, bullying can let people very bad results. I watched a movie. In the movie a person got bullied. In the end, that person died because of it. Also, when I was in elementary school, I saw a student in another class get bullied. His school work became very bad because he was bullied. These examples show bullying is very not good. I think in the end, good means being kind and not bullying others. Bullying makes people sad, lonely, and hurt. We should always be nice to others. If we are kind, the world will be better.
