What is good

Many people have different ideas about what is good. Some people think success and money are the most important things. However, I believe being good means being yourself, being kind to others, and trying to improve yourself.

First, people should not care too much about other people’s opinions. If we always try to make others happy, we may forget what we really want. For example, some students choose things because their friends like them, not because they enjoy them. A good life means making choices that are right for yourself.

Second, being kind is very important. Small actions like helping a friend or being polite can make other people happy. Kind people create better friendships and stronger relationships. When people are kind to each other, the world becomes a better place.

Finally, good people always try to improve. Nobody is perfect, but we can learn from mistakes and become better. Students study to learn more, and athletes practice to improve their skills. Trying to improve shows responsibility and hard work.

In conclusion, being good is not only about success or other people’s opinions. It means being yourself, treating others kindly, and continuing to improve. I believe these qualities help people live happier and better lives.
