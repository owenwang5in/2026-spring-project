Analysis

Conclusion

The results were similar to what we predicted. Although the majority of the respondents don’t know much about animal adoption, they care about the issue of animal adoption. They believe that policymakers should put animal adoption in the education curriculum and organize face-to-face activities with animal shelters for animals and animal adopters. In conclusion, with how much the general public cares about animal adoption and believe adding animal adoption into the education curriculum and holding face-to-face activities to be an effective way in increasing animal adoption rates, this is something policymakers could look into and consider incorporating.
