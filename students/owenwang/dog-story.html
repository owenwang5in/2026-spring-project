My Dog: Story

Buttercup lives in the neighborhood park. Yesterday he decided to sleep on a bench, and that’s where he woke up today. Buttercup gets up and goes to wander around like he always does. He goes around finding food and water, and stopping from time to time to help people because he's a gentleman like holding the door, carrying things for people, helping an elderly get across the street, etc. He occasionally stops to smell flowers and plants, he likes nature. The sun is about to set, and Buttercup is still roaming around, that’s when he notices a small group of shady guys. They are discussing something and Buttercup decides to listen in on the conversation. He overhears those guys talking about stealing from the house they're standing next to right now and they’ll do it tonight when they’re asleep.

At night, buttercup waits until everyone in the house is asleep. Then, he sneaks into the children’s room through the open window. He searches for a toy box and then drags it to the living room. Then he opens the box and then pours the toys across the floor by the entryway. Then he sneaks back outside, and walks to the entryway and hides in their front porch garden waiting for those shady guys to appear. Bad guys get in and sneak into the house. It’s all dark in the house and they can’t see a thing, but right as they close the door behind them and walk a few steps forward. The guys all slip on the toys and fall to their backs. Hearing their own thud noise they made from their falls, they hurry outside in case of the family waking up from it. Then as they hurry out of the house Buttercup barks at them and then they run away as fast as they can leaving the door open. After chasing them off, Buttercup hurries back in the house through the unclosed front door and quickly puts all the toys back in the box, then he quietly drags it back into the children's room and doing it as sneakily as possible in case of the children waking up and puts the toybox back in the place he found it. Then he goes back to the front door, closes it and goes away. Walks to the park and this time he decides to sleep next to a shrub.

The next day the family wakes up and none of them know what happened last night as they were all asleep. After eating their breakfast the kids want to play with toys and they go to the toybox and notice that the toybox is full of the scent of flowers. Buttercup goes to an alleyway this day to search for food, and then suddenly gets caught in a net. Next thing he knows he is taken to a cage and is now in an animal shelter. If you adopt Buttercup, he will be your bodyguard and protect your family. He will also be perfect for you if you happen to like nature, because Buttercup loves spending time around flowers and plants.
