My Dog: Buttercup

Name: Buttercup (毛毛)
Cage location: C11
Sex: Male
Age: 7-9 years old
Appearance: All black except white mouth area
Size: Medium
Personality: Calm, quiet, gentlemanly
Likes: Spending time around plants and flowers

Today, I'll be introducing you to Buttercup. Although he is a dog, he is a well-mannered gentleman. He has black fur that gets an iridescent shine when under the sun. He also has white fur on the area around his mouth, making it look like he’s wearing a suit. He not only dresses like a gentleman, he also acts like one. He doesn't randomly bark, and when other dogs might be running from place to place, Buttercup always walks with a steady pace. If he wants to get to a place quicker, he will instead just walk faster while still being composed.

Buttercup acts much like a bodyguard. The few times he barks are when chasing off other dogs that want to tussle. He doesn’t like other dogs that much, but he's okay with people, it just takes more time for him to get comfortable compared to some other dogs.

If you adopt Buttercup, he can become your family’s bodyguard, and he will get a family and a loving home, it’s a win-win! As you know, Buttercup is a calm dog, so it's easier and less tiring to take care of him compared to other energetic dogs. He’s also older, so you know he is more mature.
