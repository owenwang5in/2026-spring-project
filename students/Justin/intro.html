Introduction

Adstract

Many animal shelters have too many dogs. To help increase adoption rates, we did a survey. We went outside and asked people if they have been to places with dogs, like dog cafes or farms. We also asked them about the best way to go to the shelter. Before the survey, we thought shelters had too many dogs because they lacked money. But we were wrong. Many people said they had no time and were scared of dogs. In fact, more people are scared of dogs than having no time. So we need to help people not be afraid of dogs. Through this survey, we learned what people think about dogs and how to help more animals get adopted.
