Happy: The Cosmic Knight

"A story of unmatched loyalty, cosmic duties, and ultimate gentleness."

🌌 Part I: The Silent Guardian of the Coastal Highway

In the coastal town of Gongliao (貢寮), where the salty sea breeze sweeps over the asphalt, the roads are not what they seem. To the human eye, they are just ordinary lanes connecting villages. In reality, they are "time tunnels", unstable tears in the fabric of reality connecting parallel universes. When night falls, the cars speeding through the darkness aren't just passengers; they are "time travel vehicles" carrying an unknown energy capable of shattering the world’s balance.

The only thing standing between and chronological chaos is Happy (黑皮).

On the surface, Happy is just a five years old black, mixed-breed stray dog. But beneath his coat lies the soul of a cosmic guardian. Usually, Happy is as calm as a mountain, watching the world pass by with wisdom.

Whenever an outside time-travel vehicle attempts to tear through the village, Happy springs into action. He sprints after the speeding metal beasts, barking strongly to disrupt the uncontrolled energy, force the vehicles along their correct path, and protect the oblivious residents.

But humans can not see the parallel universes. To the unsuspecting villagers, he is just a dangerous, crazy black dog terrorizing drivers. Frustrated and fearful, the residents finally report him to the government.

"To the unsuspecting villagers, he was just a crazy dog. To the universe, he was its only shield."

⚖️ Part II: The Weight of Misunderstanding

For long years, Happy has guarded the village. Despite being constantly misunderstood, yelled, and driven away by the very people he protects, he has never lost heart. He silently endures the profound loneliness of his mission, choosing to love the world in his own unique way.

Whenever he encounters residents taking an evening walk, his guardian characteristic melts away. He walks over, sits by their side, and lets them pet him. When his black skin is touched, his vast energy transforms into pure gentleness and a sense of security, bringing immense peace to those around him. Through this simple touch, he absorbs and relieves the fatigue of their day.

But all of this was shattered by the arrival of unknown individuals.

One afternoon, the government notification took effect. A large, formidable truck carrying uniformed animal protection personnel arrived in the village. Seeing the massive vehicle, Happy’s instincts turned wrong, he mistook it for a giant, catastrophic time travel crisis vehicle. He began chasing, barking to correct its course, but fell straight into a specially made capture net. The doors slammed shut, and Happy was driven away.

"When his black skin is touched, his cosmic energy transforms into pure gentleness and security."

🏥 Part III: The Calm Within the Cage

Happy was brought to the Ruifang (瑞芳) Animal Shelter. Initially, all things happened standardly: the staff planned to put him through a routine health check, neuter him, vaccinate him, and release him back to his coastal territory.

However, during their days together, the shelter staff noticed something extraordinary. This black dog has an almost magical "calming aura." The shelter was normally a messy place, but whenever staff members grew anxious, overwhelmed, or agitated, Happy would carefully approach them. As they reached out to stroke his dark coat, the magic happened again. When his black skin is touched, his energy transforms into a sense of safety, bringing immense peace to those around him.

He would quietly lay down beside them, and a wave of calm would wash over the room. The staff looked at each other, realizing the truth, Happy was no ordinary stray dog.

🤝 Part IV: The Destiny

The day finally arrived to return Happy to Gongliao. The transport vehicle started its engine, its exhaust sputtering as it prepared to take the black dog back to the dangerous coastal roads.

But at the last moment, an idea appears. "The road is too dangerous for him, and honestly, we need him here. Let's keep it!"

Happy stayed. His work shifted from the dangerous high speed traffic of the highway to the concrete corridors of the shelter. Although he wasn't adopted by any human family, he found new meaning at the Ruifang Shelter. He became not only a member in the shelter, but a source of power for humans and other abandoned animals.

🛡️ Epilogue: The Knight in Room C6

Happy has officially moved into the Ruifang Animal Shelter. Having completed his sterilization and vaccinations, he has traded his solitary life on the streets for an exclusive group code name within the organization: "C6".

Happy is a true knight. He has left behind his heroic battles against monsters and temporal anomalies in Gongliao, transferring to a life of quiet, worldly gentleness in Ruifang. He has no traditional home of his own right now. Instead, he rests peacefully in the shelter, serving as the proud guardian of C6 area.

If you are willing to step into the Ruifang Shelter, you will find him there. He is waiting, not just for anyone, but for the destined one who can see past his ordinary appearance, truly understand the gentleness locked within him.
