My dog: Pink

Name: Pink
Male/Female: Female
Age: 5 to 6 years old
Descriptive words: Curious
How did the dog end up at the shelter: She was picked up by a grandma when she was little and sent it to the shelter.
Previous owner/home: A grandma
Likes/Dislikes: She doesn't like being touched by an unfamiliar person. She likes to sniff the grass and flowers.
Size: Small

I’d like to introduce a lovely dog named Pink! Her age is 5 to 6 years old. The reason why she came to the shelter is because she got picked up by a grandma when she was little and she sent it here. She is a curious girl that likes to sniff grasses and flowers when walking her. She’s nervous when she meets someone new, and she’ll protect her food very hard from other dogs. When you and Pink get to know each other better, she’ll be very loyal and will like to show her cuteness.

Adopting a dog is a logical and responsible choice. First, many dogs in shelters do not have homes, so adopting helps reduce the number of homeless animals. Second, it is usually cheaper than buying a dog from a pet shop. Third, dogs can improve your life by giving you friendship and reducing stress. I believe Pink’s cuteness can warm your heart. If more people adopt instead of buying, fewer dogs will be left without care. So why don’t you adopt our cutie Pink!

Imagine coming home after a long, tiring day, and a pair of eyes is waiting just for you. That’s Pink. She may be a little shy at new faces and new places, but deep inside, she is simply waiting for someone to show her that the world can be kind. Pink didn’t choose to lose her home, and she didn’t choose to feel afraid. But you can choose to change her story with a little patience and love, her nervousness will slowly turn into trust and loyalty. Somewhere in her small heart, Pink is still hoping for a person who will not give up on her. Someone who will see her not as “just a shelter dog,” but as family. By adopting Pink, you are not only saving a life, you are giving her a chance to finally belong, to finally feel loved.
