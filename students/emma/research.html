Learn to do research📃

This semester, I learned about research and how to design and carry out a simple study. My classmates and I worked together to create a survey, collect responses from different people, and organize the data in a clear way. After that, we analyzed the results and looked for patterns and interesting findings. It was a fun and meaningful experience that helped me improve my understanding of data analysis, teamwork, and research skills. I also learned how important it is to ask clear questions and carefully interpret information before making conclusions.
