What is Good

Introduction
Strong people who help others are good. Real strength is not about hurting people. It is about helping and protecting others.

Body
True strength means having self-control and helping others. Strong people do not bully people. They choose to do good things. For example, a strong person helps someone who is being bullied. This shows that real strength is using power in a good way.
Helping others can make society safer. When people protect each other, everyone feels safer and happier. Society becomes more peaceful.
Also, good actions can spread. When one person helps others, more people may do the same thing. For example, if one person shares food, others may start sharing too.

Conclusion
In conclusion, strong people who help others are good because they use their strength in a positive way. Real strength means helping, protecting, and being kind to others. When people do good things, society becomes a better place for everyone.
