MY DOG: Story

In the city of Sparks, something strange happened. Some dogs were born with a magical power called "Goblin." This power made them do weird things. Some could float, and some could glow.

Humans grew scared of these weird dogs. They did not want them anymore, so they threw them onto the streets. To catch these stray dogs, the city made a team called the [Sweepers].

They wore heavy clothes and used Null-Gloves. When these special gloves touched a dog, the dog lost its magical power instantly and became a normal, helpless dog. Left-left is a kind, sweet dog.

She has a Goblin power called Spark-Tail. Whenever she feels happy, excited, or full of love, her tail glows and sparks like a beautiful sparkler.
