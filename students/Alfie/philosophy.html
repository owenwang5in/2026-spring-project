What is Good?

Should we give cash to the homeless?

Many people give cash to the homeless to be kind, but is this really a good thing? First, giving money creates the wrong incentives, if sitting on the street is an easy way to get money, people will not want to find a job. In New York, some homeless people can make $40,000 a year from donations. This turns homelessness into an "easy job" and rewards people for staying on the street. And I think this is not good for a country/ ecology. We cannot control how the money is spent. We want to help buy food, but the money might be used for drugs or alcohol, which makes it harder for them to return to a normal life. Giving money does not solve the real problem. Statistics from the Ministry of Health and Welfare show that the homeless population grew by 50% from 2019 to 2024. This proves that just giving cash does not work. It only makes the population grow because surviving without a job becomes too easy.

In conclusion, true kindness is about the results of our actions. Instead of giving cash, we should support organizations that help people find real jobs and homes. Let’s be smart with our kindness to truly change lives.
