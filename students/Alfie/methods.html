Methods

Study Design

Do what before the survey, you need to know. Like watching a movie about animals, go to a place that has dogs/ cats conform to your theme, and find the best way to do your theme.

Participants

We will ask our survey of 50 people, 4 of daan students/ 46 strangers. This can let us know what people think

Materials/Tools

What we need in the survey we need question list for your survey/ mic, mic helps you to know what the interviewer says/ camera, camera helps you to make data easily/ poster, poster can let the interviewer easily see your question.

Procedure

We have 4 steps for the survey, 1: Form groups. 2: Assign jobs for our survey. We need 1 speaker to tell them our question, 1 recorder to write down what the interviewer says, 2 helpers to make sure the survey is good, and hold the poster so the interviewer can clearly see. 3: Go to a place where people can be interviewed. 4: Conducting the survey, first let the interviewer know who I am and what our project is.

Special Considerations

What do we need to do for the surveying? First: find a good place, a good place means that there are people who can be the interviewer. Second: find a person you think has time (don’t ask more than one person at the same time), speak loudly, and make sure the interviewer can hear it. In the survey, we can’t switch job/ change the introduction. Third: be respectful, respect for the interviewer, like don’t use bad words/ chase people.

Data Analysis

To analyze our data, we first process it by putting all the answers together. Then, we sort the information and make simple charts so it is easy to see patterns. Next, we look at the results and describe what they mean, keeping in mind that the findings change based on the region. Finally, we finish by writing down our main conclusions.
