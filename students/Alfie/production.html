Production Journal

We filmed every scene many times. We did this because the angles and heights were different each time. Sometimes, the camera shook too much. Sometimes, we could see shadows. We tried our own DIY stabilizer, but it did not work well. In the end, we found that holding the camera by hand was much better and more stable! 😮

Why are we DIY stabilizer? Because we though dog's first-person perspective is hard to film, after we film a lot, we can't find a perfect view. 80% is film by stabilizer 20% film by my hand.
