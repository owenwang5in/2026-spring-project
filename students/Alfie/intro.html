Introduction

Abstract

Many animal shelters have too many dogs. To help increase adoption rates, we did a survey. We went outside and asked people if they have been to places with dogs, like dog cafes or farms. We also asked them about the best way to go to the shelter. Before the survey, we thought shelters had too many dogs because they lacked money. But we were wrong. Many people chose no time and scared of dogs. In fact, more people are scared of dogs. So, we need to help people not be afraid of dogs. Through this survey, we learned what people think about dogs and how to help more animals get adopted.


Predictions

Before we did the survey, we made predictions about how people might answer each question. We wanted to compare our ideas with the real survey results later.
